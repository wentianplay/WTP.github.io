About our game

关于我们的游戏

Adventurer, civilization awaits your command!
探险者，文明等待汝的指挥！
"TianBIN" is a 2.5D strategy anime-style game themed around cultivation and technology. Here, you will command disciples and armies in battle. As an adventurer, you need to manage all the actions of your civilization. Build your city, expand your territory, and cultivate your disciples while advancing technology.
《天彬TianBIN》是一款以修真科技为主题的2.5D策略二次元游戏。在这里，你将指挥弟子和军队进行战斗。作为一名探险，你需要管理文明的所有行动。建设你的城池，扩展领土，并培养修炼和发展科技。
Enjoy real-time battles focused on strategy rather than click speed. Showcase your strategic talents by assembling armies and training disciples to conquer all opponents. You can act independently or form alliances, competing with tens of thousands of players from around the world.
享受以策略为核心的实时战斗，而非点击速度的比拼。通过组建军队和培养弟子展示你的战略才能，并征服所有反对者。你可以单独行动，也可以结盟，与来自世界各地的数万名玩家竞争。

ABOUT US

关于我们

WenTianPlay was founded on May 3, 2024. We are committed to developing strategy anime-style games with a cultivation and technology theme, with innovation and fun as our ultimate goals. Our first game, "TianBIN," has now been released.

文天玩创立于2024年5月3日。我们立志于开发修真科技题材的策略二次元游戏，创新性和趣味性是我们的终极目标。我们的首款游戏《天彬》现已面世。
When we first started developing 'Tianbin,' our primary goal was innovation. We wanted to provide both new and veteran players with an equally enjoyable gaming experience. To achieve this, we started from scratch, pushed our limits, and broke the conventional patterns seen in games currently on the market. Although the development process was full of challenges, by combining our years of development experience with player feedback, we were able to deliver a truly unique gaming experience.
最开始开发《天彬》时，我们的最核心目标就是创新。希望能够给新老玩家带来同样愉快的游戏体验。为了实现这个目标我们从零开始，挑战自我，突破了目前市面上游戏的固有模式。开发过程虽然历尽了千难万险，但是我们将自己多年的开发经历和玩家反馈相结合，真正带来了一次独一无二的游戏体验。
